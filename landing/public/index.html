Questions

Everything you might ask

How do I save Telegram files to Notion?

Forward or send the file to a chat connected to the bot. Documents, images, audio and video up to 20 MB are uploaded to your Notion page as real attachments — not just links.

Can I save messages from a Telegram group or channel?

Yes. Add the bot to any group or channel and choose to auto-save every message, or only the ones that @mention it.

Does it create duplicate pages?

No. Reply to a message you already saved and the bot updates the existing Notion page instead of creating a new one — so your database stays clean.

Which file types and sizes are supported?

Any file type up to 20 MB per file — PDFs, images, audio, video, documents and more, plus voice messages and video notes.

Does it keep my text formatting?

Yes. Bold, italics, links and line breaks are preserved when your message is saved to Notion.

Can I connect more than one Notion workspace?

Yes. Connect multiple workspaces and route different chats to different databases from a single place.

How do I connect Telegram to Notion?

Open @to_notion_robot, press Start, authorize Notion, pick a database and send a message. The whole setup takes under a minute.
